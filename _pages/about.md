I am currently Research Professor (Directeur de Recherche) at the [Centre National de la Recherche Scientifique (CNRS)](www.cnrs.fr), at the [Institut de Mathématiques de Marseille](https://www.i2m.univ-amu.fr/).

  My main research interests are in nonlinear dynamics/dynamical systems with emphasis on Hamiltonian dynamics and chaos. I consider applications to atomic, molecular and plasma physics. 

Expert-evaluator for the European Commission, working for the [European Research Executive Agency](https://commission.europa.eu/about-european-commission/departments-and-executive-agencies/european-research-executive-agency_en) (in particular for Marie Sklodowska Curie actions) and the [European Education and Culture Executive Agency](https://www.eacea.ec.europa.eu/index_en) (in particular for Erasmus Mundus programs). 

Editor-In-Chief of [Communications in Nonlinear Science and Numerical Simulation](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/communications-in-nonlinear-science-and-numerical-simulation) ([Elsevier](https://www.elsevier.com/)). 

Signatory of [DORA](https://sfdora.org/)
