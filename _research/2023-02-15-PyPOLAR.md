<img src="/images/pypolar.png" alt="PyPOLAR" width="150" height="auto"> PyPOLAR is a Python-based app for analyzing polarization-resolved microscopy data to measure molecular orientation and order in biological samples. The techniques covered include one-photon fluorescence (1PF), CARS, SRS, SHG, two-photon fluorescence (2PF) and 4POLAR 3D fluorescence. 
Version 2.4 (released on February 2, 2023) includes an ROI manager and an edge-detection module.
Available on Windows and macOS.
